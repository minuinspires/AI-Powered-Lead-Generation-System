# Requirement Analysis

## Project Title
AI-Powered Lead Generation System

## Overview
The AI-Powered Lead Generation System is a Salesforce-based solution designed to automate lead management using Agentforce AI. The system analyzes lead information, assigns lead scores, generates AI-powered summaries, and helps sales teams focus on the most promising opportunities.

---

## Objectives

- Automate lead identification and qualification.
- Improve sales efficiency through AI-driven lead scoring.
- Generate intelligent lead summaries using Agentforce.
- Reduce manual effort in lead management.
- Increase lead conversion rates.

---

## Functional Requirements

- User Authentication
- Lead Creation and Management
- AI Lead Scoring
- AI Summary Generation
- Lead Categorization (Hot, Warm, Cold)
- Email Notifications
- Validation Rules
- Salesforce Flow Automation
- Reports and Dashboards
- User Roles and Profiles

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- High Performance
- Secure Authentication
- User-Friendly Interface
- Scalability
- Reliability
- Data Privacy
- Easy Maintenance

---

## Software Requirements

- Salesforce Developer Org
- Salesforce Agentforce
- Prompt Builder
- Salesforce Lightning Experience
- Google Chrome
- Internet Connection

---

## Hardware Requirements

- Intel Core i5 / Ryzen 5 Processor
- 8 GB RAM
- 20 GB Free Disk Space
- Stable Internet Connection

---

## Stakeholders

## Expected Benefits

- Faster Lead Qualification

## Conclusion

The Requirement Analysis phase identifies the functional and non-functional requirements necessary for developing the AI-Powered Lead Generation System. By leveraging Salesforce Agentforce, Prompt Builder, Flows, and AI-driven lead scoring, the system enables organizations to automate lead management and improve overall sales performance.
