# Problem-Solution Fit

## Project Title

AI-Powered Lead Generation System

---

**Developed by – Minu Antony**

---

## Introduction

The Problem-Solution Fit phase ensures that the proposed AI-Powered Lead Generation System effectively addresses the challenges faced by organizations in managing and prioritizing sales leads. This phase validates that the designed solution aligns with business needs and user expectations.

---

## Problem Statement

Organizations often struggle with manually identifying, qualifying, and prioritizing potential leads. This process is time-consuming, prone to human error, and may result in missed sales opportunities. Traditional lead management methods lack intelligent decision support, making it difficult for sales teams to focus on high-value prospects.

---

## Proposed Solution

The AI-Powered Lead Generation System leverages Salesforce Agentforce to automate lead management. The solution uses AI-powered lead scoring, intelligent summaries, workflow automation, and real-time reporting to improve sales efficiency and decision-making.

---

## Problem–Solution Mapping

| Business Problem | Proposed Solution |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Manual lead qualification | AI-powered lead scoring |
| Difficulty identifying high-value leads | Automated lead prioritization |
| Time-consuming lead management | Salesforce Flow automation |
| Lack of intelligent insights | AI-generated lead summaries |
| Inefficient sales tracking | Reports and Dashboards |
| Inconsistent data quality | Validation Rules |
